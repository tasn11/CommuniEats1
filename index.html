About the CommuniEats app

2.3 million tonnes of edible food is wasted each year in Canada. Food waste ends up in landfillls where it breaks down to form methane, a greenhouse gas that is 86 times more powerful than carbon dioxide. The CommuniEats app saves all the perfectly good food from restuarants, bakeries, and grocery stores that gets discarded at the end of the day.
